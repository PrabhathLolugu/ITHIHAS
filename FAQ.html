Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about ITHIHAS

What is ITHIHAS? ▼
ITHIHAS (Indian Traditional Heritage Interactive Audio Stories) is a digital platform that brings ancient Indian epics and stories to life through modern storytelling techniques. Our platform combines traditional narratives with interactive elements to create an engaging learning experience.
Is ITHIHAS suitable for children? ▼
Yes! ITHIHAS is designed for all age groups. We have specially curated content suitable for children, with age-appropriate storytelling and explanations. Parents can use our platform to introduce their children to the rich cultural heritage of India.
Are the stories available in multiple languages? ▼
Currently, our stories are available in English, Hindi, and Sanskrit. We are actively working on adding more regional Indian languages to make our content accessible to a wider audience.
How are the stories selected and curated? ▼
Our stories are carefully selected from authentic sources like the Ramayana, Mahabharata, and other classical texts. Each story is reviewed by experts in Indian mythology and literature to ensure accuracy while making them accessible to modern audiences.
Can I suggest a story or character to be featured? ▼
Absolutely! We welcome suggestions from our community. You can use the contact form in your account settings to submit story or character suggestions. Our content team reviews all suggestions and considers them for future updates.
How often is new content added? ▼
We add new stories and character profiles weekly. Major updates to our epic collections occur monthly, including new interpretations, scholarly insights, and interactive features.
Are there different versions of the same story? ▼
Yes, we often provide multiple versions of the same story as they appear in different texts and traditions. This helps users understand the rich diversity of Indian storytelling traditions and regional variations.
Can I access ITHIHAS offline? ▼
Yes, ITHIHAS is available as a Progressive Web App (PWA). You can install it on your device and access previously viewed content offline. Some features may require an internet connection for full functionality.
Which devices and browsers are supported? ▼
ITHIHAS works on all modern browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge) and is optimized for both desktop and mobile devices. For the best experience, we recommend using the latest version of your preferred browser.
How do I report technical issues? ▼
You can report technical issues through our Help Center or by clicking the "Report Issue" button in the footer. Please provide as much detail as possible, including your device type, browser, and steps to reproduce the issue.
How do I create an account? ▼
Creating an account is free and easy! Click the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner, enter your email address, create a password, and verify your email. You can also sign up using your Google or Facebook account.
What are the benefits of having an account? ▼
With an account, you can: bookmark favorite stories, track your reading progress, participate in discussions, receive personalized recommendations, and sync your preferences across devices. Premium accounts get additional features like ad-free experience and exclusive content.
How can I reset my password? ▼
Click the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your registered email address, and we'll send you a password reset link. For security reasons, the link expires after 24 hours.
