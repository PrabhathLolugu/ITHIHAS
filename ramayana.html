An Ancient Epic of Duty, Devotion, and Righteousness

Overview

The Ramayana is one of the largest ancient epics in world literature, composed by sage Valmiki. It follows Prince Rama's quest to rescue his beloved wife Sita from the clutches of Ravana with the help of an army of monkeys.
