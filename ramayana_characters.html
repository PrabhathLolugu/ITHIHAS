Characters of Ramayana

Discover the divine and mortal beings who shaped the epic tale of dharma, devotion, and righteousness. From the noble prince Rama to the mighty Hanuman, explore the rich tapestry of characters that bring the Ramayana to life.

Lord Rama

Prince of Ayodhya, Avatar of Vishnu

The seventh avatar of Lord Vishnu, embodiment of dharma and ideal king. Known for his unwavering devotion to duty and righteousness.

Righteous Dutiful Compassionate

Sita

Princess of Mithila, Avatar of Lakshmi

Daughter of Earth, wife of Lord Rama, and embodiment of wifely devotion and inner strength.

Devoted Strong-willed Pure

Jatayu

Divine Vulture

The noble bird who fought Ravana to protect Sita and sacrificed his life in the process.

Noble Brave Selfless

Lakshmana

Prince of Ayodhya

Rama's devoted younger brother who accompanied him during his exile and fought by his side.

Loyal Brave Skilled Warrior

Dasharatha

King of Ayodhya

The father of Rama, known for his righteousness and the tragic dilemma he faced.

Just Wise Loving Father

Kausalya

Queen of Ayodhya

Mother of Rama, a symbol of maternal love and devotion, who plays a crucial role in Rama's upbringing.

Loving Devoted Selfless

Urmila

Wife of Lakshmana

The devoted wife of Lakshmana who stayed in Ayodhya during the exile, representing silent sacrifice.

Devoted Patient Sacrificing

Bharata

Prince of Ayodhya

The righteous brother of Rama who ruled Ayodhya as his regent, keeping Rama's sandals on the throne.

Righteous Devoted Humble

Kaikeyi

Queen of Ayodhya

Mother of Bharata, whose actions led to Rama's exile, though she later repented her decisions.

Complex Influential Remorseful

Sumitra

Queen of Ayodhya

Mother of Lakshmana and Shatrughna, known for her wisdom and sacrifice in letting Lakshmana accompany Rama.

Wise Supportive Sacrificing

Manthara

Maid of Kaikeyi

The maid who influenced Kaikeyi to ask for Rama's exile and Bharata's coronation.

Manipulative Influential Scheming

Ravana

King of Lanka

The powerful demon king who abducted Sita, leading to the great war.

Powerful Learned Proud

Mandodari

Queen of Lanka

The principal wife of Ravana, known for her wisdom and attempts to guide her husband away from his destructive path.

Wise Virtuous Devoted

Indrajit

Prince of Lanka

The mighty son of Ravana, who earned his name by defeating Indra, known for his magical warfare abilities.

Powerful Skilled Strategic

Kumbhakarna

Brother of Ravana

A powerful demon, who despite his fearsome appearance, possesses great wisdom and loyalty.

Powerful Loyal Wise

Vibhishana

Brother of Ravana

The righteous brother of Ravana who chose dharma over family loyalty and joined Rama's cause.

Righteous Wise Faithful

Maricha

Demon Shapeshifter

The demon who transformed into a golden deer to lure Rama away from Sita, enabling her abduction.

Cunning Deceptive Tragic

Hanuman

Divine Vanara

The mighty vanara who helped Rama find Sita and proved instrumental in the war against Ravana.

Devoted Powerful Wise

Sugriva

King of Vanaras

The king of the vanaras, who forms an alliance with Rama and plays a crucial role in the battle against Ravana.

Brave Loyal Strategic

Vali

Former King of Kishkindha

The powerful brother of Sugriva, known for his immense strength and complex relationship with his brother.

Powerful Complex Proud

Angada

Prince of Kishkindha

Son of Vali who joins Rama's army, proving to be a valuable ally in the war against Ravana.

Loyal Brave Strong
